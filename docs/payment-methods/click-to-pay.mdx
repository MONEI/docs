Improve the checkout experience by letting customers save their payment details for future orders with Click to Pay - a fast, secure payment method supported by Mastercard, Visa, American Express and Discover

![Click to Pay](/img/click-to-pay-preview.png)

- Improve the checkout experience by letting customers save their payment details for future orders with Click to Pay.
- Customers can choose to remember their device and browser for faster checkout.
- Click to Pay complies with industry standards for online transactions and is supported by global payment brands.

:::note
Currently Click to Pay is only available through our [Hosted Payment Page](/integrations/use-prebuilt-payment-page.mdx) and [Payment Modal](/integrations/use-payment-modal.mdx) integrations.
:::

To enable Click to Pay payments please <ContactSupport>contact our support</ContactSupport>.
