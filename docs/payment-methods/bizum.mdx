[Bizum](https://bizum.es/) is a Spanish instant mobile payment solution. Accept Bizum payments to let your customers make secure payments from the convenience of their smartphones.

Bizum is currently only available through the [hosted payment page integration](use-prebuilt-payment-page.mdx).

To enable Bizum payments for your account please contact our [Support Team](mailto:support@monei.com).
