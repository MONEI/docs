Easily accept payments directly to your PayPal Business Account

To accept PayPal payments through MONEI you simply need to connect your PayPal account in the [Settings -> Payment Methods](https://dashboard.monei.net/settings/payment-methods)

After you enable PayPal payment method it will be automatically available in [hosted payment page integration](use-prebuilt-payment-page.mdx) or you can use our [PayPal component](monei-js-api.md#moneipaypal-component) to render the button directly on your website.
